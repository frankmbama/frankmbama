> Carbon compliance SaaS platform for UK construction SMEs

> Property flood risk due diligence platform for Nigerian real estate

A climate risk intelligence tool helping diaspora buyers and local investors make informed property decisions in Nigeria — surfacing flood exposure, drainage vulnerability, and climate risk scores before purchase.

**Key Features**
- 🗺️ Flood risk mapping for Nigerian properties
- 📍 Location-based climate vulnerability scoring
- 🏘️ Due diligence reports for diaspora property buyers
- 🌧️ Historical flood data and seasonal risk analysis
